Create an account to manage local authority payments as part of Extended Producer Responsibility for packaging (EPR).

An EPR account will let {{data['local_authority']}}:

  • understand how payments are calculated
  • submit a query about the payment
  • complain about the payment amount

Discuss this with the person who approves waste data submissions for {{data['local_authority']}}.

Create {{data['local_authority']}}'s EPR account

In this one-off task you will:

  1. Create your EPR account.
  2. Choose up to two people who will manage the EPR account.

The people you choose must be responsible for:  

  • receiving payment for packaging waste costs
  • approving packaging waste data

These people will be able to add other users.

We advise reading the terms and conditions (opens in new tab) before you create your account.

What to do next

  1. Collect the names and email addresses of the people you've chosen to manage EPR payments and waste data.
  2. Set aside time to create your account.
  3. Create {{data['local_authority']}}'s account.
  4. Invite people to manage {{data['local_authority']}}'s EPR account.
