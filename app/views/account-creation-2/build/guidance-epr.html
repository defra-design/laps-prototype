Guidance Create an EPR for packaging account

Create an EPR account if your business imports, processes or supplies UK sourced packaging or packaging waste.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Enviroment Agency

Published: Published DD Month YYYY

Once you’ve created your account, you can use it to enrol for:

  • as a reprocessor
  • as an exporter
  • to report packaging data

You can apply for accreditation after you have enrolled.

! Important You need accreditation to raise and issue PRNs and PERNs.

Adding team members to your account

Once you’ve created an account, you’ll be able to invite team members to work on your behalf.
