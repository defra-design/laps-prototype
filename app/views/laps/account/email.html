As part of waste packaging recycling regulations, local authorities will receive payment for:

  • collection costs
  • disposal costs

This payment is part of the Extended Producer Responsibility for packaging (pEPR).

Create an account to manage {{data['local_authority']}}'s pEPR payments so your colleagues can:

  • understand how we calculate pEPR payments
  • submit waste data to pEPR
  • identify ways of improving efficacy and efficiency

Create {{data['local_authority']}}'s pEPR account

In this one-off task you will:

  • create an pEPR account
  • choose up to two people to manage the pEPR account

The people you choose must be responsible for managing:

  • finance relating to waste
  • waste data submissions

These people will get full admin permissions and will be able to add other users. This will let them create teams with relevant permissions to manage pEPR.

We advise reading the terms and conditions (opens in new tab) before you create your account.

What to do next

  1. Collect the names and email addresses of the people you've chosen to manage pEPR.
  2. Set aside time to create your account.
  3. Create {{data['local_authority']}}'s account.
  4. Invite people to manage {{data['local_authority']}}'s pEPR account.
