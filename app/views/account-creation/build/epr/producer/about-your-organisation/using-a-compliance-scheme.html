Are you using a compliance scheme?



What is compliance scheme?
Compliance schemes are third parties that help organisations meet the
EPR for packaging requierements.

Compliance schemes can:
  • register you
  • pay your registration fees
  • buy PRNs (packaging waste recucling notes) or PERNs(packaging
    waste export recycling notes)to meet your recygling obligations
  • report your packaging data

A compliance scheme cannot pay your waste managment fee.
